I'm a life-long learner, looking to share my passion for creativity by taking on challenges in imaginative ways. I've delivered experiences for ad agencies, small companies, and large corporations around the globe. From the humble beginning on a four-color press to animating comics to generating code, I've experienced it all creatively.

I currently work and play from Madison (Wisconsin), though I am used to working onsite or virtualy with a wide variety of teams across diverse geographies.

A fun fact about me; I enlisted at the age of 17 for the United States Army and served one tour of duty (OIF) in 2003. While in a war-zone, I received a battlefield promotion to a non-commissioned officer for my integrity, leadership, and selfless service. And yes, I used camo paint to draw flames on my truck in the middle of Iraq.
