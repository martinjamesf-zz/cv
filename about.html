About

I’m a curious intrapreneur looking to solve problems through creative solutions. The military honed my leadership skills and real-world has modeled my perspective. I enjoy working with others and empowering them to grow.

What I think about design

Design solves problems. It can manifest solutions through visual communication, a set of experiences, or digital interfaces. Our craft as designers, is select the appropriate solution or solutions to solve the a problem. We're able to take a leap off a ledge and build wings along the way.

Current work

I work for a large P&C that helps protect and restore dreams. I've had the opportunity to work with many talented individuals. Both in-house and external teams across the nation. It's been amazing!

I’m responsible for leading cross-disciplined teams through a user-centered design process. I lead and support designers in building highly functional and aesthetic experiences. I am also responsible for experience strategy, research, and process improvement. I "thinking creative" and keep solutions on brand, impactful, and deliverable.

My most significant contribution has been leading the charge for creating design standards. Why is this important? Our products were inconsistent with the expectation of customers and partners. The root of our inconsistencies was the avoidance of love and evolution of our standards. So, we developed a road map to change the digital landscape. First, we altered our customer-facing brand site, www.amfam.com. Second, we consolidated our energy on our self-service portals. Third, create a system that creates a more efficient and cohesive product ecosystem.
